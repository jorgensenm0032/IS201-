North Carolina

I served my misson in North Carolina. Here in Utah I'm Maren but to the people of NC I'm Sister Jorgensen. North Carolina is pretty much my favorite place on earth and to me NC is sacred ground.

My favorite places in NC:

  1. Topsail Island
    • The beach!
    • Fabulous ward that LOVES missionary work
    • Amazing sunsets
  2. Sanford
    • Friendliest people!
    • Small town feel with the necesities of a big city
  3. Albertson
    • Teeny tiny town
    • Some of the cutest, most southern people in NC
  4. Raleigh
    • Big city with TONS of people to talk to
    • The best senior missionaries live there!
  5. Pittsboro
    • LOVES sister missionaries
    • Historic downtown called "brick city"
  6. Whispering Pines
    • Right next to Fort Liberty
    • I met some of my best friends here!
